# Junior AI/ML Engineer (NLP) — Northlake AI

## About the role

Northlake AI is an early-stage startup building a customer-support automation platform
for mid-market SaaS companies. Our product reads support tickets, drafts replies, and
routes escalations, all grounded in each customer's own documentation. We are a team of
nine and we are hiring our first dedicated junior ML engineer to work alongside two
senior researchers on the retrieval and generation layer of the product.

This is a hands-on role for someone early in their career who wants to ship models that
real users touch every day, not run experiments that sit in a notebook. You will be
paired with a senior mentor for your first six months.

## What you'll do

- Build and evaluate text-classification models that tag and prioritise incoming support
  tickets by topic, sentiment, and urgency.
- Own our retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) pipeline's data-prep stage: chunking,
  embedding, and indexing customer documentation into our vector store.
- Write evaluation harnesses that measure answer groundedness and flag hallucinated
  responses before they reach a customer.
- Collaborate with the two backend engineers to expose your models behind a low-latency
  inference API.

## Requirements

- 0–2 years of professional or internship experience in machine learning, data science,
  or a closely related software role. New graduates with strong project portfolios are
  encouraged to apply.
- Solid Python fundamentals and comfort with the scientific stack: NumPy, pandas, and at
  least one deep-learning framework (PyTorch preferred, TensorFlow acceptable).
- Working knowledge of core NLP concepts: tokenisation, word and sentence embeddings,
  transformer architectures, and standard text-classification metrics (precision, recall,
  F1).
- Hands-on experience, from coursework or projects, fine-tuning or prompting at least one
  pretrained language model (BERT-family, or an open LLM such as Llama or Mistral).
- Familiarity with SQL and the ability to pull and clean your own training data.
- Clear written communication — you will document every model you ship.

## Nice to have

- Exposure to vector databases (pgvector, Pinecone, or Weaviate) and RAG patterns.
- Experience with experiment-tracking tools such as Weights & Biases or MLflow.
- A public GitHub, Kaggle, or blog presence showing NLP work.
- Familiarity with Docker and basic CI.

## Logistics

- Location: Toronto, Canada. Hybrid — two days per week in our downtown office, three
  days remote.
- We sponsor Canadian work permits for exceptional candidates but cannot support US visa
  transfers at this stage.
- Full-time, permanent. Compensation is CAD 85,000–105,000 base plus early-stage equity.
- Start date is flexible; we can wait for a new graduate finishing a spring term.

Northlake AI is an equal-opportunity employer. We evaluate every applicant on the
strength of their engineering and their curiosity, and we read every take-home submission
ourselves.
