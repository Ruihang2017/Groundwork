# Senior AI Engineer, LLM Applications — Brightseam

## About the role

Brightseam builds an AI writing assistant for legal and compliance teams. Our product
drafts contracts, redlines clauses against a company's playbook, and answers policy
questions with citations back to the source document. Accuracy and traceability are
everything — a hallucinated clause is not a cute demo failure, it is a liability.

We are hiring a senior AI engineer to own the LLM application layer: the prompts, the
retrieval, the agent orchestration, and the evaluation systems that keep quality high as
we ship weekly. This is an applied role for someone who thinks in terms of the whole
system around the model, not just the model weights.

## What you'll do

- Design and maintain our retrieval-augmented generation pipeline over customer contract
  repositories, including chunking strategy, hybrid search, and re-ranking.
- Build and tune the prompt and tool-calling logic for our multi-step drafting and
  review agents.
- Own our offline and online evaluation systems: LLM-as-judge harnesses, groundedness and
  citation-accuracy metrics, and regression suites that gate every prompt change.
- Work with domain experts (practising lawyers on staff) to encode their review playbooks
  into structured, testable behaviour.
- Manage cost and latency trade-offs across model providers.

## Requirements

- 5+ years of software engineering experience, with at least 2 years building production
  applications on top of large language models.
- Deep, practical experience with the modern LLM application stack: RAG, prompt
  engineering, function/tool calling, and structured-output extraction.
- Hands-on experience designing LLM evaluation systems — you know why "it looked good in
  the demo" is not a quality bar, and you have built the harnesses that replace it.
- Strong Python and API-integration skills; comfort working across at least two model
  providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, or open-weight models served in-house).
- Experience with vector search and at least one vector store (pgvector, Pinecone,
  Weaviate, or similar).
- A healthy scepticism about model output and an instinct for building guardrails.

## Nice to have

- Experience in legal tech, compliance, or another domain where correctness is
  non-negotiable.
- Familiarity with agent frameworks and multi-step tool-use orchestration.
- Front-end skills (TypeScript, React) to prototype your own eval dashboards.

## Logistics

- Location: Remote-first within the UK and EU time zones, with an optional London office.
- We sponsor UK Skilled Worker visas for candidates already eligible; we cannot sponsor
  relocations from outside the UK/EU for this role.
- Full-time. Base salary GBP 90,000–115,000 plus equity.
- We ship to production every week, so a pragmatic, iterative mindset matters more here
  than a perfect research pedigree.

Brightseam is an equal-opportunity employer and builds its team from a wide range of
