# Senior Full-Stack Engineer — Quillcove

## About the role

Quillcove makes project-management software for creative agencies — think shared
timelines, asset review, and client approvals in one place. We are a profitable,
bootstrapped company of about 60 people, and our product team ships user-facing features
that our 8,000 customer teams use every day. We care a lot about craft: fast pages,
thoughtful interactions, and a codebase that stays pleasant to work in.

We are hiring a senior full-stack engineer to join a product squad and own features
end to end, from database schema to the pixels the customer clicks.

## What you'll do

- Build user-facing features across the whole stack — data model, API, and interface —
  and own them from design review through production and iteration.
- Collaborate closely with a product designer and a product manager in a small,
  autonomous squad; you will have real input into what gets built, not just how.
- Improve front-end performance and accessibility, keeping our pages fast and usable for
  every customer.
- Write and maintain the automated tests that let us deploy multiple times a day with
  confidence.
- Mentor mid-level engineers through code review and pairing.

## Requirements

- 5+ years of full-stack web engineering experience shipping and maintaining production
  products.
- Strong TypeScript across the stack, with deep React experience on the front end and
  Node.js (or a comparable server runtime) on the back end.
- Solid relational-database skills — schema design, query optimisation, and safe
  migrations against PostgreSQL.
- A real front-end craft sensibility: you care about performance budgets, accessibility
  (WCAG), and interaction detail, not just making the feature appear.
- Experience designing and consuming HTTP or GraphQL APIs and reasoning about their
  evolution.
- A pragmatic testing discipline across unit, integration, and end-to-end layers.

## Nice to have

- Experience with Next.js and modern React server components.
- Familiarity with real-time collaboration features (websockets, CRDTs).
- An eye for design and comfort working directly in Figma with designers.

## Logistics

- Location: Fully remote within the United States or Canada, across any time zone that
  overlaps North American business hours by at least four hours.
- We do not currently sponsor work visas; you must be authorised to work in the US or
  Canada.
- Full-time. Base salary USD 150,000–185,000 (or CAD equivalent) plus profit-sharing.
- No dedicated on-call; the whole team shares a light support rotation during business
  hours.

Quillcove is an equal-opportunity employer and is proud of the range of backgrounds on
its engineering team.
