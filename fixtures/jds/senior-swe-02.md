# Senior Infrastructure / Platform Engineer — Vantage Cloud

## About the role

Vantage Cloud runs the internal developer platform for a 400-engineer product company.
Our team's customers are our own engineers: we own the Kubernetes clusters, the CI/CD
pipelines, the observability stack, and the paved-road tooling that lets a product team
ship to production without becoming infrastructure experts. When we do our job well, a
new service goes from empty repository to production in an afternoon.

We are hiring a senior platform engineer to help us scale that paved road as the
engineering organisation grows, with a particular focus on our multi-cluster Kubernetes
footprint and developer self-service tooling.

## What you'll do

- Own and evolve our Kubernetes platform across multiple regions and clusters, including
  cluster upgrades, capacity planning, and workload isolation.
- Build self-service tooling and internal APIs that let product teams provision
  databases, queues, and services safely without filing tickets.
- Improve our CI/CD pipelines for build speed, reliability, and safe progressive
  delivery (canary and blue-green rollouts).
- Own key reliability metrics for the platform and drive down toil through automation.
- Partner with security to bake guardrails — network policy, secrets management, image
  scanning — into the paved road rather than bolting them on.

## Requirements

- 5+ years of infrastructure, platform, or SRE experience running production systems at
  scale.
- Deep, hands-on Kubernetes experience — not just deploying to it, but operating,
  upgrading, and debugging clusters.
- Strong infrastructure-as-code discipline with Terraform (or an equivalent) and
  experience with a major cloud provider (AWS, GCP, or Azure).
- Proficiency in a systems or scripting language for tooling — Go strongly preferred,
  Python acceptable.
- Solid understanding of CI/CD, container build systems, and progressive-delivery
  patterns.
- Experience with observability at scale — Prometheus, Grafana, and distributed tracing.

## Nice to have

- Experience building an internal developer platform or golden-path tooling.
- Familiarity with service meshes (Istio, Linkerd) and policy engines (OPA).
- On-call and incident-command experience for platform-wide outages.

## Logistics

- Location: Remote within Canada, or hybrid from our Vancouver office.
- Applicants must already be authorised to work in Canada; we are not sponsoring visas for
  this role at this time.
- Full-time. Base salary CAD 155,000–190,000 plus equity.
- Platform on-call rotation, roughly one week per month, with time off in lieu.

Vantage Cloud is an equal-opportunity employer and welcomes candidates from all
backgrounds and career paths.
