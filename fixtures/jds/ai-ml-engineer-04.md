# Staff Machine Learning Engineer, Applied Research — Meridian Labs

## About the role

Meridian Labs is the applied-research group inside a large logistics company. We sit
between pure research and product engineering: we take promising ideas from the
literature, prove they work on our real data, and hand production-ready prototypes to
the platform teams. Our current focus is large-scale forecasting and combinatorial
optimisation for routing millions of packages a day.

We are hiring a staff-level ML engineer to set technical direction for the forecasting
research track and to raise the engineering bar across a team of ten researchers. This
is a senior individual-contributor role with heavy technical-leadership expectations and
no direct reports.

## What you'll do

- Define the research agenda for demand and travel-time forecasting, balancing ambitious
  bets against the platform's near-term needs.
- Personally prototype and validate new modelling approaches — probabilistic forecasting,
  graph neural networks over the road network, and hybrid ML/optimisation methods.
- Set standards for experimental rigour: how we design offline evaluations, how we guard
  against leakage, and how we decide a result is real before it reaches production.
- Act as the primary technical reviewer for the team's most consequential design
  documents and mentor senior researchers toward staff level.
- Partner with product and platform leadership to translate research wins into roadmap
  commitments.

## Requirements

- 8+ years of experience in machine learning, with a track record of taking research
  ideas all the way to production impact at scale.
- Depth in at least one of: time-series and probabilistic forecasting, graph neural
  networks, or large-scale optimisation. Breadth across several is a strong plus.
- Expert Python and strong software design instincts; you set the patterns others follow.
- A record of technical leadership without formal authority — driving cross-team
  technical decisions, writing influential design docs, and mentoring senior engineers.
- Rigorous experimental methodology: you can spot a leaky evaluation or an over-fit
  benchmark from across the room.
- Excellent written communication for a technical audience — much of a staff engineer's
  leverage is on the page.

## Nice to have

- Publications at top ML venues (NeurIPS, ICML, KDD) or equivalent applied impact.
- Experience with logistics, supply chain, or operations research.
- Familiarity with distributed training and large-scale data processing (Spark, Ray).

## Logistics

- Location: Seattle, Washington, or remote within the US Pacific and Mountain time zones.
- We sponsor and transfer visas, including H-1B and O-1, and support permanent-residency
  applications.
- Full-time. Base salary USD 230,000–275,000 plus a substantial equity grant and annual
  research-conference budget.
- Occasional travel to our Seattle hub for quarterly research summits.

Meridian Labs is an equal-opportunity employer committed to an inclusive research culture.
